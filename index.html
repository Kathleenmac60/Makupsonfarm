About Makupson Farming LLC

Makupson Farming LLC is a multi-member LLC operating in Marshall County, Mississippi. We combine row crop production with livestock enterprises to reduce risk and support steady cash flow across seasons. Our approach emphasizes land stewardship, operational planning, and strong financial discipline.

Operations Plan

Land Use

  • Cotton: ~20 acres
  • Corn: ~15 acres
  • Livestock & infrastructure: ~4 acres

Annual Cycle

  • Winter: planning, maintenance, financial review
  • Spring: planting, fertilization, livestock setup
  • Summer: crop management, herd/flock care
  • Fall: harvest, marketing, pasture rotation

Practices

  • Routine soil testing and nutrient planning
  • Crop rotation (cotton/corn) to support soil health
  • Biosecurity and herd health protocols
  • Custom hire used when economical
  • Conservative reserves and insurance participation

Crops

Cotton

Cotton is our primary cash crop. We plan production seasonally, manage fertility and weeds, and market through local/regional gins.

Corn

Corn provides flexible marketing options and can support livestock feeding when appropriate. We manage fertility based on soil tests and rotate as needed.

Livestock

Chickens

  • Egg production for local sales
  • Secure housing with predator protection
  • Feed management and sanitation protocols

Goats

  • Meat goat production
  • Pasture management and brush control benefits
  • Fencing, shelter, and water access improvements
